About Me


My Life Story I was born on November 15, 2007, a date that marks the beginning of a journey filled with growth, effort, and ambition. From an early age, I was drawn to discipline and challenge—two qualities that would define much of my life.

During my teenage years, I actively participated in football, a sport that taught me far more than just physical endurance. Through teamwork, strategy, and relentless practice, football instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, time management, and resilience under pressure—skills that would later translate into other areas of my life.

At the age of 15, I set myself a new challenge: to master the English language. Over the course of two dedicated years, I immersed myself in learning, practicing daily, and pushing my boundaries. This commitment paid off when I achieved an IELTS score of 6.5, a milestone that reflects not only language proficiency but also consistent hard work and focus.

Nowadays, I’ve channeled that same energy and discipline into learning frontend development. I spend my days consistently deepening my understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern tools, driven by a passion for building real, functional digital experiences. I know that mastery takes time, but I also know—through football, through English—that time and effort always pay off.

This is only the beginning of my journey, but each chapter so far has shaped who I am today: someone who works hard, learns consistently, and keeps aiming higher.
